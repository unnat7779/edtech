Question 1: [MCQ] [PHYSICS] [MEDIUM] [CHAPTER: Mechanics]
A particle moves in a circle of radius 10 cm with constant speed. If it completes one revolution in 2 seconds, what is its angular velocity?

A) π rad/s
B) 2π rad/s  
C) π/2 rad/s
D) 4π rad/s

Correct Answer: A
Explanation: Angular velocity ω = 2π/T = 2π/2 = π rad/s

Question 2: [NUMERICAL] [PHYSICS] [HARD] [CHAPTER: Mechanics]
A car accelerates from rest at 2 m/s² for 5 seconds. Find the distance traveled in meters.

Correct Answer: 25
Explanation: Using s = ut + (1/2)at², where u=0, a=2, t=5: s = 0 + (1/2)(2)(25) = 25 m

Question 7: [NUMERICAL] [MATHEMATICS] [MEDIUM] [CHAPTER: Calculus]
Find the derivative of f(x) = x³ + 2x² - 5x + 3 at x = 2.

Correct Answer: 19
Explanation: f'(x) = 3x² + 4x - 5. At x = 2: f'(2) = 3(4) + 4(2) - 5 = 12 + 8 - 5 = 15

Question 10: [NUMERICAL] [PHYSICS] [MEDIUM] [CHAPTER: Thermodynamics]
Calculate the heat required to raise the temperature of 2 kg of water from 20°C to 80°C. (Specific heat of water = 4200 J/kg°C)

Correct Answer: 504000
Explanation: Q = mcΔT = 2 × 4200 × (80-20) = 2 × 4200 × 60 = 504,000 J

Question 12: [NUMERICAL] [MATHEMATICS] [HARD] [CHAPTER: Integration]
Evaluate the definite integral ∫₀¹ x² dx.

Correct Answer: 0.333
Explanation: ∫x² dx = x³/3. Evaluating from 0 to 1: [1³/3 - 0³/3] = 1/3 ≈ 0.333
